About TM School

Thami-Mandisa School of Azania is more than a school; it's a movement towards empowering the youth of Africa. Founded by Nhongo Solo, a passionate educational, youth, and community activist, the school embodies the spirit of hands-on learning and practical empowerment.

Nhongo Solo, a Politics, Philosophy & Economics graduate and proudly African trailblazer, brings her commitment to education and community to every aspect of the school. She believes in equipping students not just with knowledge but with the tools to shape their futures.

At Thami-Mandisa School of Azania, students graduate with a practical portfolio and a recognized certificate, setting them apart as innovators and changemakers in their fields. Join us in shaping the future of Azania—one student at a time.




Why Should You Choose TM School of Azania?


Thami-Mandisa School of Azania is not just an institution—it's a heartfelt endeavor rooted in love, family, and community. Born out of a shared vision for a brighter future, TM reflects the spirit of Ubuntu, where every student is nurtured, celebrated, and empowered.
